Mango juice

A milkshake is a sweet, cold drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings into a thick, creamy mixture. It evolved from an early alcoholic beverage into the popular, non-alcoholic treat it is today, with the invention of the electric blender significantly boosting its popularity in the early 1900s.

Ingredients

  • MILk
  • Mango
  • Suger
  • Alcohol

Instructions

  1. 1/3 cup milk
  2. 1 mango
  3. 1/2 Alcohol
  4. 200 gram suger

instructions

  1. Soften the ice cream and chill the glass. Take the ice cream out of the freezer for 10 to 15 minutes before use
  2. Measure the ingredients. 1 cup ice cream to 1/4 cup milk is a great ratio.
  3. Blend the shake. In a blender, combine the milk and ice cream and blend until smooth. ...
  4. Serve
